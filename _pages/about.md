I am an Undergraduate AI Researcher completing my B.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering (Artificial Intelligence and Data Science) at the Sikkim Manipal Institute of Technology (SMIT). 

My core research focuses on building hardware-efficient deep learning architectures, sequence modeling frameworks, and uncertainty quantification systems.

### Current Research Engagements
* **Joint Research Intern** at the Indian Statistical Institute (ISI) Kolkata & University of Salford (UK) under the supervision of Prof. Umapada Pal and Prof. Shivakumara Palaiahnakote.
* **Focus Area:** Bypassing traditional segmentation bottlenecks for scene text recognition and image restoration in the wild.

### Research Themes
* **State Space Models:** Optimizing 2D-Mamba architectures for spatial and temporal modeling.
* **Medical Imaging & Remote Sensing:** Developing robust frameworks resistant to distribution shifts and data leakage.
* **Computer Vision:** Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs), Vision Transformers, and Deep Delta Learning (DDL) applications.
